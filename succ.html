Our Story

Founded in 2015, Frescor began with a simple passion for bringing people together through memorable events. What started as a small team of event enthusiasts has grown into a premier event management company known for creativity, precision, and exceptional service.

Over the years, we've had the privilege of planning and executing hundreds of events, from intimate weddings to large-scale corporate conferences. Each event has taught us something new and helped us refine our approach to create truly exceptional experiences.

At Frescor, we believe that every event tells a story. Our mission is to help you tell yours in the most beautiful, engaging, and unforgettable way possible.
